0x90: NoteOn

Remarks.

2016.12

Art&Technology Conference, Exhibition

Abstract.

“Creation Beyond Imagination.”

It is the strongest power of illusion that we work off our creative energy eventhough we can’t realize that in the constraints of life. We want to represent the moment that people can experience the power of illusion, using the light in the space.

People will touch the light bulbs - the main objet which has the light in the limited shape - and make sounds. With a line of 8 light bulbs and their sounds, we can feel another harmony. ‘0x90’ is a MIDI command which means ‘Note On’ and in this artwork, we all become performers who make sounds from the light and design multimodal interaction that stir each other’s various feelings.
